Problems Identified

Maintaining Friendships Under the current situation with the pandemic, many families are unable to meet up with each other for a gathering. Originally, there already aren't a lot of chances for extended family members to meet up with each other, and the pandemic will only strain this distant relationship further and further.
Family Reunions Because of the pandemic, schools are often closed and students are attending online classes. As a result, there may be little to no communication between friends. Furthermore, the government issues social restriction orders, which limits the amount of friends that can meet up together physically.
Cybersecurity Without many activities to take part in during the pandemic, most students tend to spend their time on social media. However, most students are not aware of the different dangers they may face online. For example, we have heard about many incidents related monetary scams and leakage of personal data.
Social Media Without the ability to do so physically, some students may try to make new relationships online. However, these online relationships are very dangerous, futile and fragile. Online relationships can terminate in a fraction of a second. With a click of a button, people can do whatever they want without consequences.
